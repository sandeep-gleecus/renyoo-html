HOW IT WORKS

Renyoo - more than an image annotation app!

Pictures are integral to our lives. People, places, moments and memories. People close to our hearts, places of our dreams, moments of celebrations and memories of past. Put all together and there’s the story of our lives.

But there’s more you can do with your beautiful pictures than pinning them in your social media walls or storing them into oblivion in your folders.

Renyoo adds life to your pictures. It helps you annotate pictures. Add text, images, audio, video and watch them come to life. Add tags for quick search & discovery, GeoTag them and Share securely or download it for offline viewing - you’ve everything to make your pictures timeless and rediscover favorite memories.

And there's more...

Renyoo can be a valuable companion in your day-to-day life. Be it adding more details to your shopping lists, taking detailed notes in a meeting to discussing work with your colleagues using images - Renyoo does all that.

Annotate & highlight your pictures.
Add Hotspots!

Want to highlight an area, object, person or context in your pictures?

Just add a hotspot to it with a label. You can zoom in while adding hotspots to focus on a particular area of the image.

Add text, audio, and videos to your images.
Enrich them.

Want to add more contextual information to your hotspots?

Add text, URL, audio, images and video to your hotspots. Let your pictures tell their stories.

A picture with a collection of hotspots is called a POD.

Share Photos

Share pictures securely.

Want to share your images but are worried about its security? Share your PODs via a URL on email or social.
